Get Involved!

There are several ways to get involved with the group. It's a great way to give your resume a boost or pickup a new skill. Here are some options:

  • Come to an event! Come hang out with us and meet people in the area doing work in the data visualization field. We always have excellent speakers, food, and swag. Best of all, most of our events are free! Join us on meetup.com and RSVP for the next event.
  • Help us maintain this site! We need help generating content, fixing bugs, and adding features. Check out our current list of issues on GitHub and contribute where you can.
  • Come speak at an event! We always need speakers. Check out our speakers page for tips on what topics are appropriate.
